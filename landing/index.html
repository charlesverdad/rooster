Features

Everything you need to
manage your volunteers

From creating rosters to tracking availability, Rooster handles the scheduling so your team stays organised.

📅

Roster Creation

Build weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly rosters in minutes. Set up recurring schedules that repeat automatically.

👥

Team Management

Organise volunteers into teams with roles. Invite members via email or share a link — they join in one tap.

🔔

Push Notifications

Volunteers get notified instantly when assigned. No more chasing people on group chats or missed messages.

✅

Accept & Decline

Volunteers can accept or decline assignments with a single tap. You see responses in real time on the dashboard.

📅

Availability Tracking

Members mark dates they’re unavailable. When you create rosters, you always know who’s free.

📱

Works Everywhere

Use Rooster on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. Install it as an app or use it in your browser.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Three simple steps to get your team rostered and notified.

1

Create Your Team

Sign up, create a team, and invite your volunteers. They’ll get an email or link to join.

2

Build a Roster

Pick a schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — and assign volunteers to roles for each event.

3

Everyone Gets Notified

Assigned volunteers receive a push notification. They accept or decline, and you see it all on your dashboard.
